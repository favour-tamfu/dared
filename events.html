Our Events & Activities

Explore our recent projects and community engagements dedicated to promoting rights and equity.

A Visit to the Bafut Palace

A meeting with local producers of the #Atogho at the Bafut Palace, a UNESCO Tentative list heritage site, to discuss boosting the local creative sector.

World Heritage Volunteers

Partnering with the Regional Delegation of Arts and Culture for the UNESCO World Heritage #VOLUNTEERFUND project at the Bafut Royal Palace.

Commemorating World Heritage Day

A day of activities including a radio visit and tours of heritage sites like the German fort and Menteh Waterfall with university students.

Youth Empowerment Workshop

Skill development especially amongst youths is an integral part of building a more resilient futur and One of the most sustainable ways of building local economies.

Film for Advocacy Session

At the Embassy in Yaounde seeing young and aspiring film makers from all over Cameroon gain skills on film making in general and film making for Advocacy.

World Theater Day

Celebrating WorldTheatreDay2024, Direct Action for Rights Equity and Development in partnership with the Regional Delegation of Arts and Culture Northwest and Noble Arts Entertainment brought together youths, young and aspiring actors from the Region.
